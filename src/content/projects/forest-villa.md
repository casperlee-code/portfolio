The Forest Villa is an architectural exploration of raw concrete and natural shadows. Located in a dense forest, the structure is designed to integrate into its environment by reflecting the verticality of surrounding trees.

The interior spaces are defined by a rhythmic sequence of concrete portals, creating a gradual transition from public spaces to intimate sanctuaries. Large openings allow natural light to wash over the rough-textured walls, shifting throughout the day to reveal the passage of time.
