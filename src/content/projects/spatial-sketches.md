A series of experimental hand sketches exploring structural lines, tectonic junctions, and spatial transitions. These drawings investigate the relationships between support and load, solid and void, structure and enclosure.

Each sketch is drawn with black ink on textured paper, emphasizing the clarity of the lines and the direct expression of architectural ideas before they are translated into digital forms.
