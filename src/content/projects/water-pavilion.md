Hovering over a calm lake, the Water Pavilion is a meditative space designed for quiet contemplation. The structure uses a simple grid of steel columns supporting a dark timber roof, creating an open frame that captures the horizon.

During sunset, the warm golden light reflects off the water surface, casting moving patterns onto the underside of the canopy. The design blurs the boundary between interior and exterior, nature and human intervention.
