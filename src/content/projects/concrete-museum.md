This gallery space is designed as an architectural canvas for contemporary art. By removing all decorative elements, the architecture itself becomes a silent backdrop, allowing the artworks to speak.

A single skylight splits the ceiling, casting a sharp, moving beam of light across the main concrete wall. This interaction between sunlight and concrete transforms the space into a dynamic art installation in its own right.
