About

Vision, Context, and Tectonics

Hello, I'm Casper (Ping-Kuan) Lee, a passionate architecture student from Taiwan. My architectural practice focuses on 'Context' and 'Tectonics', balancing sensibility in ideation with rationality in execution. I have explored architecture across various scales—from curation at the AA Visiting School to professional internships at STOA Architects in the U.S., as well as Harmony Design and AS Structure in Taiwan. I constantly challenge my potential to turn the extraordinary into everyday spaces, broadening my architectural understanding through diverse cultures and continuous exploration.
