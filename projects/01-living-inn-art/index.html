School Project — 2025

Living Inn Art

專案概述

“Living Inn art” integrates a community gallery, artist residence, and themed hotel. Its open ground floor faces the Greenway, naturally drawing visitors into the site. The upper floors include artist studios that open as exhibition halls and guest rooms with small workshops for hands-on artistic experiences.

Site Location: West District, Taichung Keyword: Inclusive Arts Village Tools: Revit, AutoCAD, D5 render, Photoshop, Illustrator

設計理念：Reversing the Perception of Art

In our daily lives, has the connection between art and life become too distant? Art is originally a translation of life, expressing the artist’s interpretations shaped by different life experiences. Yet, art often feels profound, difficult, and isolated as an experience. This makes me wonder: could spatial design help make art a natural part of everyday life and create accessible spaces for artistic exchange? Therefore, in Taichung’s West District — an area rich with art communities — I hope to create a space where local residents, artists, and travelers can engage with art without boundaries, fostering sharing and inclusiveness.

空間策略：Reversing the Regular Room Unit 從 Isolated 到 Inclusive

Artist Room: Add on a 2~4m space outside the regular room unit. When the artists leave, their living and working spaces can become exhibition areas for the public to better understand their daily creative life. Traveller Room: Keep the privacy & foster the interaction at the same time. I added balconies and studio spaces to the rooms, giving travelers opportunities to step outside and display their work.

Inclusive Art Village It aims to overturn the common perception of art as distant, difficult, and isolated by fostering an accessible space for openness, interaction, and creative exchange.

A. Artist private residence: The window openings in the private residential rooms are more closed off. B. Gallery exhibition Space: 45 degrees angled walls create a strolling experience. C. Art plaza: The roadside second floor demarcates the Art Village’s open space. D. Traveller room: The guest room balconies also serve as art displays. E. Artist room: It is both a creative studio and an artist exhibition hall.
